For Chandigarh

Fly into Indira Gandhi International (DEL)
  • FlyDirect flights into Delhi run from most major cities. Indira Gandhi International is the gateway.
  • Then trainFast trains reach Chandigarh in roughly 3–3½ hours.
  • Or taxiAround 4–5 hours by road, door to door.
  • Or fly onShort connecting flights reach Chandigarh in about an hour.

For Athens

Fly into Athens International (ATH)
  • FlyDirect flights into Athens run from across Europe and beyond, year-round.
  • Into townAirport metro and taxis reach the centre in about 40 minutes.
  • DetailsVenue and area notes will follow with the Athens invitation.

Once your tickets for India are booked, please feel free to message Sanjula for help with booking a train or taxi.
